What is our mission?

This Text Does Not Exist is the revolutionary approach to produce text - not from a human, but a computer. Use our Transformer-driven or LSTM models to generate text from three contexts: random generation, storytelling from a user-inputted seed, and question-answering. Our goal is to democratize AI, so check out our About pages to get a functional understanding of LSTMs, RNNs, Transformers, and word embeddings.
