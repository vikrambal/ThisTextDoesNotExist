Summary

You can do as much with this program as you wish. It's an excellent program if your primary goals are to increase explosiveness and increase general strength. You can see that the workout is set around "compoumd lifts," like squats, bench press, and cleans - which reap the most benefit because of how many muscle groups they target.

If you would like an elite training program (and have accesss to a gym with the necessary equipment), feel free to give this a shot! If not, this is a good resource for gauging difficulty in terms of a single workout, finding individual exercises that work for you, or just having a foundation for you to develop your own routine.

Remember - the power is in your hands! But don't worry, there's no weight on your shoulders to do anything (unless you happen to be doing back squats in which case you should readjust the bar to rest on your upper traps - little joke there!) Have fun, and rock out!
